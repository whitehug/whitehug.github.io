Areas of Interest

Mod Development

I spend a good portion of my free time modding video games such as The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim. A lot of time goes into looking into file conflicts and coming up with resolution using a large variety of tools. It's all worth it when everything comes together for a unique and interesting game experience. I intend to eventually create my own mods for the game when I find something that I think is missing and should be implemented.
I also plan on making smaller projects, such as a mock Advance Wars or tools I can use on my phone to help me run a game of Dungeons & Dragons with my friends.
